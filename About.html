ABOUT US

MISSION STATEMENT

The Heist Series started out as a passion project to bring the hype of Tekken tournaments to North West Marietta. Our target audience is young adults who play Tekken at any level from competitive to casual and are well off enough to compete on a weekly basis regardless of gender and have atleast finished high school.
